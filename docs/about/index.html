About Me

By definition I am a story teller. I am a statistician by training and data scientist by passion. I am inherently drawn to the art of storytelling through data.

Python is my main language of choice due to its easy to read syntax and more importantly, it extensive libraries for data cleaning, manipulation, statistical analysis and machine learning model build such as pandas, sci-kit learn and Tensorflow.

Additionally, I analyze data with Excel and Power BI, using PivotTables, Power Query, and DAX to uncover insights and create dynamic visualizations. For database management and extractions, transformation and loading (ETL) tasks, I rely on SQL Server Management Studio and Azure.

Embarking on an exciting journey, I'm shifting gears from financial advisory to dive deep into the fascinating world of data science, fueled by a passion for uncovering insights and driving innovation

Why Data Science

All in all I love working with data and analyzing it to find interesting tid bits that may not readily be recognizable at first sight. As an Arsenal fan and a fan of the English premier league, I find it fascinating how teams with lesser resources use the Moneyball theory to stay competitive with teams that have much bigger budgets and bettter financed squads. Similarily, Data Science enables businesses with smaller teams and finite resources to be able to better compete and gain a tactical advantage by leveraging data to maximize on their USP.
