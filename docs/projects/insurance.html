Insurance Claims Analysis

This project focuses on applying data science techniques to sales functions within an organization. It outlines customer segmentation, customer lifetime value analysis, and sales forecasting to enhance marketing, guide customer retention strategies, and enable data-driven business decisions. The proposal utilizes Python and R along with libraries like Pandas, Scikit-learn, and TensorFlow to cluster customers, predict future revenue, and build forecasting models.
